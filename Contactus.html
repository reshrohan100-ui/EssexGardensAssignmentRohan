EGT is a registered charity run entirely by volunteers who care about gardens and landscapes at every scale; from heritage landscapes, to private gardens and public roadside verges. We understand that they all matter and have a value; not only to the quality of our environment, but also to the quality of our lives.

If you would like to become a member or want to help us with research or admin, then do please join us. (A membership application page will open in a separate download. Please print, fill out and post to the address on the page. We look forward to welcoming you.)

We are an informal and very friendly bunch, and happy to have a chat.
